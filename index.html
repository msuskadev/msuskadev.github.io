Definition of Hype Driven Development


Making decision about software architecture or technology stack based on inaccurate opinions, social media and in general on what is considered to be "HOT"

Calculate initial cost

Answer questions

What is the problem I am trying to solve by adding this new technology to the stack?

Will the use of this technology pay back the cost of the entrance curve and rewriting?

What's the cost of convincing and training other team members?

Are my team members tend to hype?

Will they respond with enthusiasm or with skepticism?

Am I able to take up the role of Evangelist for specified technology?

Do I know how to measure and analyze the risk of adding new piece of technology to the stack?
